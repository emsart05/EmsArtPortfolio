Hunting Race
In the aunnual Ferret Hunting Race, participants will being their own gun and race to shoot as many ferrets in 3 hours as they can. The participant to shoot and kill the most ferrets will be determined the winner. The winner will recieve $500 in a format of their choice (gift card, cash, check, etc.).
To sign up, call the number on our contact page linked here.

The Hunting Race will be held on:
June 15, 2022 at Wayne National Forest
ALL participants must have a legal hunting license.
